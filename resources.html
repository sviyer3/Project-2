What is Implicit Bias?

Implicit bias refers to unconscious attitudes or stereotypes that affect our understanding, actions, and decisions. These biases operate automatically and can influence behavior even when we consciously reject prejudice.

What is the IBT?

The Implicit Bias Test (also known as the Implicit Association Test or IAT) measures automatic associations between concepts (like race or gender) and evaluations (good or bad).

Try it here: https://implicit.harvard.edu/implicit/

Intersectionality

Intersectionality describes how overlapping identities (race, gender, class, sexuality) shape individual experiences of privilege and oppression.

Why This Matters in College

Understanding implicit bias and intersectionality helps create inclusive classrooms, equitable policies, and respectful communities.
