Why This Project Matters

Technology isn't neutral: design decisions can amplify stereotypes, hide certain voices, or make systems feel “normal” for some people and unsafe for others. This project connects course ideas about intersectionality and implicit bias to real online experiences.

"Small design choices can produce big outcomes, especially at scale."

Shrinath Iyer, Jassi Chahal, and Yilin Gao
